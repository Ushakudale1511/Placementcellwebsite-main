Training and Placement Department

Padambhooshan vasantraodada patil Institute of technology Budhgaon ,sangli

The training and placement cell of the college is very effectively engaged in developing the students in all needed skills and placing in top companies. Students are also apprised on a continuous basis through seminar and workshops etc. about higher learning opportunities in India and abroad. Right from the fourth semester onwards, regular training is conducted on total personality development and personal grooming of the students. Minimum 35 to 40 top companies visit to the college every year between 90% to 100% students are placed. The placement covers all branches equally including non IT branches Mechanical, Civil, IEM, Biotechnology etc and a good number of non IT companies also visit to college on a continuous basis along with IT companies. The training and placement of the college is headed by an MBA(HR) qualified training placement Director with 28 years of experience on HR/HRD related activities tasks.

About Us

The Training & Placement Cell of Sir PVPIT is fully established, well equipped and very effectively engaged in grooming/shaping the career of the students by inviting top to top companies to college and preparing students through needed soft skills, Holistic development of their personality, inculcating the tempo of professionalism and bridging the gap between academic learning & Company expectations to keep the students employable at all the times.



  • PROF S
  • M.Sc., MBA, DHRM, ADIM, PGDPM & IR
  • Director: Training and placement
  • 35 Years of experience in soft skill training and entire HRD related activities.
  • As eminent soft skill trainer, Delivered many useful talks on topics like change management, “Role of HRD in higher Education” etc on FM channel under GYAN VANI programme.

The T&P Cell of PVPIT is headed by Prof S H Khan—Director—T&P, a very experienced HRD professional and Expert soft skill Trainer with 38+ years of vast quality experience on HRD related activities. The entire soft skill Training to our students is imparted/inculcated by Director: Training and placement himself through 7 phased Training module created by Director himself. The placement Scenario of the college is extremely good. The average Placement across the branches ranges between 75% to 100% and 35 to 50 select Top companies visit to college every year. We wish to update with extreme satisfaction that despite acute CORONA crisis and lock down scenario, we could manage to place 71% students of 2020-21 batch (when corona was at its dangerous peak) in 38 top companies and 89% students of 2021-22 batch were placed in 47 companies.
